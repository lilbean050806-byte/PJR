Bob's Red Mill Flour Brand Re-Design
For this project, I redesigned the visual identity and branding strategy of an existing consumer product, following a full end-to-end branding process from research to final campaign execution.
Date: Feb 5, 2026
Client: School Assignment
Service: Brand & Logo Re-Design

Things I did

I rebranded Bob’s Red Mill Flour by conducting research and competitor analysis to identify gaps in its positioning and visual identity, then developing a refined brand strategy focused on a clearer target audience and modernized messaging. I translated this into a cohesive visual system, including updated packaging, logo direction, and typography, using AI tools to explore and refine design concepts. Finally, I created a social media campaign with original video content to extend the brand across digital platforms, resulting in a complete branding package that strengthens consistency, improves user experience, and enhances the brand’s appeal to contemporary consumers.
